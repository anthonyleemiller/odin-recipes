Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is an amazing recipe for lasagna. If you want to channel your inner Garfield, then you are going to want to eat this lasagna...

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients
  2. Heat to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Serve and enjoy!
